Design Build/Assist Services

Collaborative delivery. Predictable results.

From concept to completion, NLine Electric partners with owners, engineers, and contractors to deliver efficient, well-coordinated electrical systems.

Design-Build provides a single-source solution—integrating engineering and construction under one team to streamline communication, accelerate schedules, and reduce project risk.

Design-Assist brings our field and construction expertise into the design phase. By working alongside the design team, we provide real-time budget guidance, constructability feedback, and value engineering while the Engineer of Record maintains final design authority.

Regardless of delivery method, our focus remains the same: efficient execution, cost control, and electrical systems built for long-term performance.

Why this version works well in proposals

It subtly highlights things owners and general contractors look for:

  • Collaboration with owners, engineers, and contractors
  • Schedule acceleration
  • Risk reduction
  • Constructability review
  • Cost control
  • Long-term system performance
