About N Line

Who We Are

We're N Line Electric, a Colorado-based industrial and medium voltage electrical contractor supporting upstream oil & gas operations, industrial plants, and mission critical data center infrastructure throughout Northern Colorado and Southern Wyoming.

With more than 50 years of combined experience, our team specializes in medium voltage electrical systems up to 35,000 volts (35kV and under), delivering safe, reliable power distribution for production facilities, industrial operations, and mission-critical data center environments where uptime is essential.

Our primary customers include upstream oil & gas operators, industrial plant owners, data center developers, and infrastructure managers who require experienced crews, strict safety standards, and precise execution in demanding operating environments.

Core services include:

  • Medium voltage power distribution systems (600V–35kV)
  • Overhead and underground electrical distribution installations
  • Medium voltage cable installation and terminations
  • Metal-clad switchgear installations and upgrades
  • Medium voltage switchgear and transformer installations
  • Utility interface and service coordination
  • Electrical maintenance, outages, troubleshooting, and emergency response

N Line Electric is committed to quality workmanship, code compliance, and long-term partnerships in environments where reliability, safety, and continuous operation are non-negotiable.

More About N Line Electric

Since 2003 we've been providing high-quality industrial and medium voltage electrical contracting with a strong emphasis on customer service.
