Safety is our Promise

Electricity can be pretty dangerous. An electrician must always be aware of potential risks in electrical systems. A well-trained and knowledgeable electrician can easily identify and assess risk factors and generate solutions to avoid them.

We have a culture of safety at N Line Electric; we believe that safety is paramount to the services we provide. Without proper safety precautions, electrical systems can be harmful and potentially life-threatening. Everything an N Line electrician does works toward minimizing these risks and optimizing safety.

Safety Partners

We work with these organizations and services to keep the sites where we work safe and secure:

  • N Line Electric maintains employee safety with DISA Global Solutions
  • PICS Auditing is a third party safety monitoring company we work with for some of our oil and gas clients.
  • We work with ISNetworld to ensure safety for some of our oil & gas clients.
N Line Electrical Contractors:
  • Are NFPA 70E Trained and re-certified every year.
  • Are trained in Hazard Communications, Safeland Training, Lock-Out/Tag-Out, Aerial Lifts, and Fall Protection.
  • Have attained Cost Containment Certification with the State of Colorado
