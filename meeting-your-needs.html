Meeting Your Needs

Your business and your needs are our top priority. When you work with N Line, you're adding another member to your team. We devote the same amount of attention and care you might expect from your most loyal employee to each and every job.

Consistent Staff

We're proud of our low employee turnover rate, and the valuable client service it allows us to offer. You're likely to work with the same contractor from N Line for years to come. You'll have continued, consistent and knowledgeable service from a familiar face.

Safety-Minded

We have developed a culture of safety at N Line Electric. We continuously train all of our staff in the latest safety techniques, hold daily meetings, and even provide our workers with their own personal protective equipment (PPE). Our employees are committed to performing all of their work with a strong focus on safety.

Training & Education

Our experienced electricians are the reason our clients keep coming back. We attract and retain the best and brightest in our industry by offering training, continued education, and competitive salaries throughout their career. N Line's employees are the key to our company's success.

Licensed & Certified

We are licensed contractors in both Colorado and Wyoming.

  • N Line Electric electricians are IECRM trained - Trained by Independent Electrical Contractor's Rocky Mountain
  • Our electricians receive OSHA-certified NFPA 70E training - OSHA-approved NFPA 70E Safety Training Certified
  • We have received Colorado State's Department of Labor and Employment's Cost Containment Certification - Cost Containment Certification from the State of Colorado

All of N Line's Apprentice Electricians complete IECRM's 4-year training program, and our apprentice linemen are enrolled in IECRM's Merchant Job Training & Safety program.
