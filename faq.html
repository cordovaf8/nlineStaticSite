FAQ

Common questions about switching plans and service.

Will my power go out when I switch?

No—switching providers doesn’t interrupt delivery service.

How long does switching take?

Most enrollments complete in 1–2 billing cycles (varies by area).

Do you charge cancellation fees?

Some fixed-term plans may have early termination fees; details are shown at signup.

Can I go paperless?

Yes—paperless billing is available in your account portal.

Who do I call for an outage?

Call your local utility for outages. We can help with billing questions.

Do you have 24/7 support?

Yes—our support line is available 24/7.
