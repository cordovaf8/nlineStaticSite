More About N Line Electric

Since 2003 we've been providing high-quality electrical contracting services with a strong emphasis on customer service. The number one goal for N Line Electric is to make all of our customers happy through quality workmanship, dependability, and our willingness to put in the extra effort for any and every client.

N Line Electric is prepared for anything: our electricians are well-equipped – with both the right tools and the right training.
